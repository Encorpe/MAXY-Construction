About
MAXY Construction

Maxy Construction Ltd founded in 2015.
Our company has built up an enviable reputation for its work within the industry, which includes our restoration work on ISG project in Kew Gardens, Royal Botanic Gardens, UNESCO world heritage site.

Our employees have experience in installation of metalwork in various leading projects such as commercial offices, private luxury apartments, shopping centres, airports terminals, train stations and offshore platforms.

Maxy Construction Ltd confirms to all EU Health and Safety at work regulations and operates to highest standards in the work place. We are proud to state we have none accidents happened from the date the company has been established.

The company mission is to ensure our clients receive outstanding quality products and innovative ideas in fitting of architectural and structural metalwork.

We are looking forward for new challenges and projects.

Experience in architectural and structural metalwork is required. You must be capable to assist site fitter or steel erector. CSCS card required. Other construction related cards or certificates will be considered as an advantage.
